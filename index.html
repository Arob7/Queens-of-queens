Queens of Queens

Catherine of Aragon

First wife of English ruler Henry VIII and mother of Mary Tudor (Bloody Mary).

Anne Boleyn

Second wife of English ruler Henry VIII and mother of Elizabeth I. She was married to Henry for only three years before being beheaded based on (false) charges of adultry, incest and sorcery.

Jane Seymour

Third wife of English ruler Henry VIII and mother of King Edward VI. She later died of complications due to childbirth.
