Queens of Queens





Catherine of Aragon

First wife of English ruler King Henry VIII and mother of Mary Tudor (Bloody Mary).

Anne Boleyn

Second wife to King Henry VIII and mother of Elizabeth I. She was married to Henry for only three years before being beheaded based on (false) charges of adultery, incest and sorcery.

Jane Seymour

Third wife of King Henry VIII and mother of King Edward VI. She later died of complications due to childbirth.

Anne of Cleves

Fourth wife of English ruler Henry VIII. Anne never had any children with Henry VIII. They were only married six months before the marriage was annulled.

Katherine Howard

Fifth wife of English ruler King Henry VIII. She was the youngest of all the wives being married at just 16 or 17 while King Henry was almost 50. They were only married for 18 months before she was beheaded for treason and adultery.

Catherine Parr

Sixth wife of English ruler King Henry VIII. She was the last surviving wife and outlived her husband.
